Brisket

Description

Juicy, beef brisket that will make everyone's mouth water.

Ingredients

  • 1 packer brisket
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Sprinkle salt all over brisket
  2. Leave in fridge overnight
  3. Remove from fridge and season with pepper
  4. Smoke for 1 hour per pound at 225 degrees F
  5. Let rest 1 hour
  6. Slice and serve
