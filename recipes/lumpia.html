Lumpia

Description

Filipino eggrolls that will have everyone saying 'Salamat'.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground sausage
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • carrots
  • green onion
  • eggroll wrappers
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Cook ground sausage in pan until medium
  2. Add carrots and onions in
  3. Once done, place onto wrappers and roll
  4. Deep fry rolls until skin is golden brown
  5. Let cool and enjoy
