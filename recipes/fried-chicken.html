Fried Chicken

Description

Crunchy, hot and juicy chicken that will be the major hit of any party.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds of chicken, preferably dark meat
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • flour
  • peanut oil

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika
  2. Sous vide at 160 degrees F for 2 hours
  3. Coat in flour and additional seasonings
  4. Fry in peanut oil at 450 degrees F until skin glden brown
  5. Remove and let rest
  6. Enjoy
